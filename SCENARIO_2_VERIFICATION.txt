- AI Mode: ON

TOU PLAN (for comparison):

✓ Solar→Battery: 0.0% (no solar excess - all solar used for day load)

ULO PLAN (with AI Mode ON):
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
✓ Annual Savings: $1,697 (much higher than TOU - AI Mode working!)
✓ Solar Direct: 30.0% (same as TOU - 3,000 kWh directly offsets day load)
✓ Solar→Battery: 0.0% (correct - no solar excess because solar = 30% of usage, dayLoad = 50%, so all solar goes to day load, leaving 0 excess)
✓ ULO→Battery: 50.0% (AI Mode enabled - battery charges from grid at ULO rate!)
✓ Grid: 20.0% (much lower than TOU - AI Mode significantly reduces grid usage)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

VERIFICATION:

Why Solar→Battery is 0%:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- Day Load = 10,000 kWh × 50% = 5,000 kWh
- Solar Production = 3,000 kWh
- Solar to Day = min(3,000, 5,000) = 3,000 kWh (all solar used)
- Solar Excess = max(3,000 - 5,000, 0) = 0 kWh
- Therefore: No solar excess available to charge battery
- This is CORRECT behavior per FRD Step B

Why ULO→Battery is 50%:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- With AI Mode ON, battery can charge from grid at ULO rate
- Night Load = 10,000 kWh × 50% = 5,000 kWh
- Battery can charge up to 5,000 kWh from grid (limited by nightLoad)
- Battery discharges to offset expensive periods
- 50% of usage offset by ULO-charged battery = 5,000 kWh / 10,000 kWh
- This is CORRECT behavior per FRD Step C (C2: Grid → Battery)

Why Savings are Higher with AI Mode:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- TOU: $794 savings (battery only charges from solar, limited capacity)
- ULO + AI Mode: $1,697 savings (battery charges from grid at cheap ULO rate, full capacity)
- Difference: $903 extra savings from AI Mode
- This makes sense because AI Mode enables full battery utilization

Why Grid Remaining is Lower:
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
- TOU: 70% grid (only 30% solar offset, no battery grid charging)
- ULO + AI Mode: 20% grid (30% solar + 50% battery = 80% offset)
- This is CORRECT - AI Mode significantly reduces grid dependency
